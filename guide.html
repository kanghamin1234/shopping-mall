User Guide

1

Sign Up & Login

Create an account to enjoy full access to Luxury Lobby. Members can track orders, write reviews, and manage their profile easily.

2

Browse Products

Explore our exclusive collection in the 'Shop' page. You can filter items by category or search for specific products using the search bar.

3

Add to Cart

Found something you love? Click 'Add to Cart' on the product list or detail page. You can review your selected items in the Cart page at any time.

4

Checkout

Review your order in the Cart page and click 'Order Now'. Your order will be processed immediately, and you can check the status in 'My Page > Order History'.
